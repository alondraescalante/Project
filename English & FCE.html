Important Values

Equity and Equality

Equity is when a person or thing have specific opportunities according to its condition. For example, in a soccer game to see what’s happening and there are some levels to stand up there, a tall person won’t need that, but a person that is not very tall will need at least one of these levels.

Instead, equality is when all the people is exactly the same without taking about its condition, all the people have the same opportunities. For example, in the case of the soccer game, all the people will have a level, no matter if it is tall or short.

Nowadays, both of these situations we can find them in social media, in the news, in our jobs, etc.… Now the most important or notable case is the gender inequality, where the women can´t participate in some politic positions or can’t receive education “because they are women”.

Ability, Capacity and Potentiality

And now we are going to talk about this three points. At the beginning we can think that they are the same, but no, they have something different.

Potentiality is the possibility of someone doing something in the future. In other words, is when we know we can do some thing but we haven’t tried yet.

Capacity refers to the ability or power to do, experience or understand something. Is when we can do an activity but we are not experts in that yet.

Ability, a skill that a person has in a determined activity, when we are already experts in that activity, we have talent, skill or professionalism in that particular area.
